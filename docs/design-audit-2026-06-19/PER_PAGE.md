## Dashboard
**Route:** `/dashboard`

**Overall:** The Dashboard is a read-only routing overview (ADR-0029) composed of CounterBar + KpiCard tiles + PortfolioTable + RosterSummary. Filters/Export/Forms are N/A by design (overview, not a list-edit surface). Net: visually and structurally on-system, but it needs the portfolio list migrated to DataGrid and the loading/error states migrated to the banded HexagonLoader to be standard-compliant.
